# 🧱 SP-BREAK — SMASH EVERYTHING! 🎮

## HEY STERLING! 👋

This is YOUR game. You get to smash sushi, candy, and ice cream with YOUR FACE! 🏓💥

**Play it here:** 👉 https://niveknow.github.io/SP-break/

---

## 🎯 WHAT IS THIS?

It's Breakout — but WAY cooler. Instead of a boring white ball, **YOUR FACE** bounces around the screen and smashes delicious food bricks!

### 🧱 The Bricks You Smash:

| Brick | What It Is | Hits To Break | Sound |
|-------|-----------|:-------------:|:-----:|
| 🍣 | **Sushi** — soft and squishy | 1 hit | *pop!* |
| 🍬 | **Candy** — hard candy shell | 2 hits | *sparkle sparkle!* |
| 🍨 | **Ice Cream** — frozen solid | 3 hits | *THUD!* |

### ❤️ Lives
You get **3 hearts**. Drop the ball and lose one. Lose 'em all and it's **GAME OVER!**

### 🏆 High Score
Your best score saves right in your browser. Can you beat it? 😤

---

## 🎮 HOW TO PLAY

```
🖱️ Desktop → Mouse to move paddle, click to launch
📱 iPhone  → Finger to move paddle, tap to launch
```

**Goal:** Break ALL the bricks to clear the level. Each level gets harder!
