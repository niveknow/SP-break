## 🎯 WHAT IS THIS?

It's Breakout — but WAY cooler. Instead of a boring white ball, **YOUR FACE** bounces around the screen and smashes BIG FOOD EMOJIS! No boring boxes — just pure food. 🍣🍬🍨

### 🍣🍬🍨 The Foods You Smash:

| Food | What It Is | Hits | Sound | Damage Look |
|------|-----------|:----:|:-----:|:-----------:|
| 🍣 | **Sushi** — soft & squishy | 1 | *pop!* | Gone instantly! |
| 🍬 | **Candy** — hard shell | 2 | *sparkle!* | Gets a CRACK line |
| 🍨 | **Ice Cream** — frozen solid | 3 | *THUD!* | Crack X when almost broken |

### ❤️ Lives
You get **3 hearts**. Drop the ball and lose one. Lose 'em all and it's **GAME OVER!**

### 🏆 High Score
Your best score saves right in your browser. Can you beat it? 😤

## 🎮 HOW TO PLAY

```
🖱️ Desktop → Mouse to move paddle, click to launch
📱 iPhone  → Finger to move paddle, tap to launch
```

**Goal:** Break ALL the bricks to clear the level. Each level gets harder!

## 📦 The Technical Stuff (for nerds)

| What | How |
|------|:---:|

| Sound | Web Audio API (synthesized) |

| Ball Avatar | Sterling's face (rendered from image) |

```

├── 📄 bricks.js     ← Brick grid & types
├── 📄 score.js      ← Points, lives, levels
├── 📄 audio.js      ← Sound effects!
├── 📁 assets/       ← Images & stuff
```
